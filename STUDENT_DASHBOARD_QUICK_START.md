# Student Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## What's New?

Your personalized student dashboard provides:
- ✨ **Real-time performance tracking**
- 🎯 **Personalized action items**
- 📊 **Visual progress indicators**
- 🏆 **Gamified achievements**
- ⚠️ **Risk assessment with confidence scores**

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Academic Health Score (Top Left)
**What it shows:** Overall academic performance (0-100)

**How to read it:**
- 🟢 **Green (≥75)**: Excellent! Keep it up
- 🟡 **Amber (60-74)**: Good, but room for improvement
- 🔴 **Red (<60)**: Needs attention - focus required

**What to do:** Use this as your primary metric to track progress

---

### 2. Attendance Card (Top Middle)
**What it shows:** Your current attendance percentage

**Target:** ≥75% is good | ≥90% is excellent

**Quick Tips:**
- Don't skip classes
- Communicate absences in advance
- Missing ≥25% affects your final grade

---

### 3. Fee Status Card (Top Middle-Right)
**What it shows:** Fee payment progress

**Status Meanings:**
- ✅ **All Cleared**: Everything paid
- ⚠️ **In Progress**: Payment pending
- ❌ **Pending**: Complete payment urgently

**Action:** Contact accounts office if stuck

---

### 4. Risk Assessment Card (Top Right)
**What it shows:** Overall risk evaluation

**Risk Levels:**
- 🟢 **LOW**: You're in good shape
- 🟡 **MEDIUM**: Monitor your progress
- 🔴 **HIGH**: Immediate action needed

**Confidence Score:** How sure we are (higher = more certain)

---

### 5. Subject Performance (Wide Card)
**What it shows:** Performance in each subject

**5 Subjects Tracked:**
1. Mathematics
2. Physics
3. Chemistry
4. English
5. CS/Economics

**Read it:** Each circle shows marks (0-100)

**Quick Goals:**
- 🎯 Aim for ≥60 in all subjects
- 🌟 Target ≥80 for strong performance
- 💯 Get 100 for perfect scores

---

### 6. Action Items (Left Side, Lower Half)
**What it shows:** To-do list personalized for YOU

**How to use:**
- ☑️ Check off as you complete items
- 📌 High-priority items highlighted
- ⚡ Address red/orange items first

**Examples:**
- "Improve Attendance" → Attend all classes
- "Boost Academics" → Study sessions
- "Complete Fee Payment" → Contact accounts
- "Join Study Group" → Find 4+ classmates

---

### 7. Study Tips (Right Side, Lower Half)
**What it shows:** Actionable study recommendations

**Key Tips:**
1. **📚 Daily Review** - 30 min daily note review
2. **⏰ Time Management** - 25 min focus + 5 min break (Pomodoro)
3. **👥 Peer Learning** - Study with classmates weekly
4. **🎯 Goal Setting** - Break into weekly milestones

**Use these:** Implement 1-2 tips this week

---

### 8. Achievements & Badges (Bottom)
**What it shows:** Unlocked and locked badges

**Available Badges:**
| Badge | Unlock Requirement |
|-------|------------------|
| 🌟 Attendance Star | ≥90% attendance |
| 🎯 High Scorer | ≥80% average marks |
| 📈 Rising Star | ≥70% average marks |
| 💯 Perfect Subject | Any subject = 100% |
| 🏆 All-Rounder | ≥70% marks AND ≥75% attendance |
| 💪 Consistent | ≥60% average marks |

**How to unlock:** Meet the requirements shown

## Quick Actions

### If You See RED (High Risk)
1. ⚠️ Read the specific factors causing HIGH risk
2. 📞 Talk to your mentor/advisor
3. ✅ Complete action items immediately
4. 📚 Increase study hours on weak subjects
5. 💰 If fees are issue, contact accounts

### If You See AMBER (Medium Risk)
1. ⚡ Don't panic - you have time to improve
2. 📅 Create weekly study schedule
3. 👥 Join a study group
4. 📝 Focus on lowest-performing subject
5. 💪 Increase effort gradually

### If You See GREEN (Low Risk)
1. 🎉 Great! Keep up the momentum
2. 📈 Target to improve to excellent level
3. 🏆 Work towards locked badges
4. 👥 Help other students in your class
5. 📚 Explore advanced topics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if my Health Score seems wrong?**
A: It's calculated from all 5 subjects. Check each subject mark is correct.

**Q: Why is my risk "HIGH" when marks look ok?**
A: Risk considers all 3 factors together. Low attendance or pending fees = higher risk.

**Q: Can I improve my confidence score?**
A: Yes! Improve the factors causing medium/high risk. As they improve, confidence increases.

**Q: How often does the dashboard update?**
A: Data refreshes every 5 seconds from the server.

**Q: What if I disagree with my marks?**
A: Contact your teacher or department office for mark verification.

**Q: How can I unlock more badges?**
A: Meet the criteria shown for each badge. Focus on consistency!
